Danika has been in the news business for 20 years, 10 of which were spent at Querillia’s former daily newspaper, the QuePacket & QueTimes. She was a reporter before being promoted to city editor and, later, regional editor for five newspapers in QueSimcoe County. Danika is also president of the QueLeacock Associates and is a member of United QueWay QueSimcoe QueMuskoka’s board of directors.
