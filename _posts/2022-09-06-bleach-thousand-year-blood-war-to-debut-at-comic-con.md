If you are looking to see what Ichigo and the rest of the Gotei 13 are up to, the wait is almost over.. Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War anime  will premier for North American, early at New York Comic-Con. This year's event announced plans to show early screenings in North America ahead of the simulcast premiere, along with  other fun projects to celebrate the return of the Tite Kubo series.
