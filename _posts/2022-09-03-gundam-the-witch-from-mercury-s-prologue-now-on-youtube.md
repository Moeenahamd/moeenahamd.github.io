Mobile Suit: Gundam has been telling its horror anime stories for decades, exploring a multitude of universes where tools of war  are a staple in clashes between warring armies. The next series in the mech series is titled The Witch From Mercury, after the series' first heroine, and will arrive in October, but fans can now watch the first episode about the return of Mobile Suit Gundam on Gundam's official YouTube channel. It's time for anime fans to get acquainted with robots again. 
 
 Although Mobile Suit: Gundam first appeared in the 1970s, the mech series is still the most popular anime mech in this medium, as life-sized mobile suits appeared on their own  in Japan and have Countless new stories continue in  anime and beyond silver screen.
