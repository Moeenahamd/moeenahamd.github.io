New York, NY - Jan 22, 2024 - Jujutsu Kaisen's long-awaited return has sent fans buzzing, particularly amidst the ongoing Culling Game arc and the looming showdown between Yuji Itadori and Sukuna. But amidst the hype, another major development has rocked the fandom: the confirmed death of series antagonist Kenjaku.

**From Mastermind to Fallen Star:**

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/062b6cfe563e54d73bd5556c6fc5bad7/b2f91a1e1595db0c-4f/s500x750/fb06500867193a628d357349b61e3b1c1f0bfae9.gifv)

Kenjaku's role in Jujutsu Kaisen cannot be understated. He's been the puppet master behind major events like Gojo's sealing and the ancient sorcerer resurrection, earning him the mantle of ultimate antagonist for many. His cunning and intellect rivaled even Sukuna's, making him a truly captivating villain.

However, his seemingly unstoppable reign met its end in the Culling Game, thanks to an unexpected duo: comedian sorcerer Takaba and the powerful Yuta. Takaba's bizarre cursed technique ensnared Kenjaku, leaving him vulnerable to Yuta's swift decapitation.

**Is He Truly Gone?**

![](https://i.imgur.com/oPBCE5x.png)

Kenjaku's demise sparked both shock and disappointment among fans. Many expected a grander exit for such a prominent villain, and the seemingly anti-climactic nature of his defeat left many feeling underwhelmed. The ease with which Yuta dispatched him, despite Kenjaku's known ability to body-hop, further fueled the doubt.

**Sukuna Takes the Mantle:**

![](https://i.imgur.com/lmUKqFS.png)

While Kenjaku may be gone, his legacy lives on. The Culling Game's control has shifted to Sukuna, who possesses his own motivations for the merger ritual. This unexpected twist adds a layer of intrigue to the narrative, as fans grapple with the implications of Sukuna's leadership and the potential clash between his goals and Kenjaku's original plan.

**Yuta Steps Up to the Plate:**

![](https://i.imgur.com/IsFmDVC.png)

Yuta's presence on the battlefield alongside Yuji and Rika further confirms Kenjaku's demise. Knowing Yuta's awareness of Kenjaku's body-hopping technique, his willingness to engage Sukuna directly signifies his confidence in Kenjaku's complete eradication.

**A Controversial Departure:**

![](https://i.imgur.com/tGcAP1T.png)

Although Kenjaku's death serves to progress the plot and shift power dynamics, the execution of his demise hasn't resonated well with all fans. Many feel his potential was squandered, robbed of a deservedly epic conclusion. This mixed reaction highlights the challenges of crafting satisfying endings for complex villains like Kenjaku.

**Looking Ahead:**

![](https://i.imgur.com/uHnm2pZ.png)

With Kenjaku officially laid to rest, the spotlight falls on Sukuna and the Culling Game's endgame. Will he fulfill Kenjaku's vision, forge his own path, or face defeat at the hands of Jujutsu Kaisen's heroes? Chapter 249, releasing on February 4th, 2024, promises to shed light on these burning questions and potentially offer redemption for the controversial handling of Kenjaku's final act.
