Dragon Ball Super - January 26, 2024 - "Dragon Ball Super" fans are buzzing over stunning fan art that reimagines the monstrous Cell Max from the "Super Hero" movie achieving the ultimate Perfect Cell form.

On Reddit, user /PMatty73 shared earlier artwork by Indonesian artist adb3388 (ade.bw_ on Instagram) depicting "Perfect Call Max." While the movie presented Cell Max resembling the original Cell, albeit in a less developed "Semi-Perfect" state, this artwork envisions him reaching the iconic peak of power and intelligence. The contrast between Max's initial brutishness and this chillingly composed Perfect form highlights a chilling "what-if" scenario.

adb3388's imagination doesn't stop there, with additional pieces showcasing Perfect Cell Max facing off against Gohan in his Beast form and even a "Super Hero" cast vs. tournament-style image, reminiscent of the beloved "Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi" fighting game series.

Speaking of fighting games, fans recently had their hopes dashed regarding a potential new "Budokai Tenkaichi" title. An elaborate fake leak claiming a release in August 2023 surfaced online, only to be debunked by the culprit themselves.

Meanwhile, the "Dragon Ball" universe continues to expand. The upcoming "Dragon Ball Daima" anime takes inspiration from "Dragon Ball GT" by rejuvenating iconic characters like Goku to their child forms. Additionally, the English release of the "Dragon Ball Super" manga recently sparked controversy over a potential mistranslation in Gohan's battle with Cell Max.

And on a lighter note, a dedicated fan ushered in 2024, the year of the dragon, with a hilarious low-budget cosplay of the legendary wish-granting dragon Shenron.

Whether it's fan art reimagining iconic characters or new adventures awaiting on the screen, the "Dragon Ball" universe continues to capture the hearts (and imaginations) of fans worldwide.
