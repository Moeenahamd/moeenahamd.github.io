**San Francisco, CA** - Jan 22, 2024 - Thrill-seeking One Piece fans, prepare to delve into the past! Eiichiro Oda's rarely seen, 1994 manga "[Monsters](https://www.viz.com/shonenjump/chapters/monsters)" finally receives its official English release courtesy of [Viz Media](https://www.viz.com/). This exciting news coincides with the recent critically acclaimed anime adaptation, further fueling demand for Oda's early work.

**A Glimpse into Oda's Roots:**

![](https://i.imgur.com/5f7orzI.png)

"Monsters" offers a unique window into Oda's burgeoning talent before One Piece's epic voyage. The one-shot follows Ryuma, a Wano Country swordsman on a quest to locate the enigmatic "King." Along the way, he joins forces with a determined woman seeking to vanquish a mythical dragon.

**Connecting to the One Piece Universe:**

![](https://i.imgur.com/Tfiq4hC.png)

While initially a standalone story, "Monsters" later became woven into the One Piece tapestry during the Thriller Bark arc. Ryuma's reemergence established Wano's place in the story and cemented his status as a legendary swordsman.

**Fans Rejoice: English Release and Deeper Connections:**

![](https://i.imgur.com/4IjKw01.png)

For One Piece enthusiasts, "Monsters" presents a treasure trove of connections. Beyond offering a glimpse into Oda's creative evolution, it strengthens the bond between Ryuma and the series' beloved swordsman, Roronoa Zoro. In 2023, Oda confirmed their familial ties, further deepening the lore.
