After almost a year of waiting, the team behind JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is back safely. Today Netflix released the next series of Stone Ocean episodes, so you can marathon them as soon as possible if you want. And trust us, you'll want to check it out. 

For those who need more information, Netflix has released episodes 13-2. This marks the second episode drop of the series to date. Currently, reports from Japan suggest a third will follow as Stone Ocean nears its end. 

Of course, if you're not familiar with Stone Ocean, the story is one of many in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. Written in 1999, creator Hirohiko Araki crowned Stone Ocean as the  sixth installment of the franchise and it has 17 episodes. David Productions began working on Jolyne's series shortly after her success with Wrapped Golden Wind. The  first 12 episodes of the show were released in December 2021 and you can watch them on Netflix. 

For a brief summary of Stone Ocean, continue reading below. 

"Florida, USA, 2011. Jolyne Cujoh was charged and sentenced to 15 years in prison after being in a car accident with her boyfriend. A pendant in  her hand as a strange power awakens within her and events unfold. inexplicable succession. While visiting, her father Jotaro Kujo reveals a terrifying truth and mentions a man named Dio. Will Jolyne Cujoh one day free herself from this "rock ocean" that is the prison? In the midst of this, the centuries-old feud between Dio and the Joestars turns sour. "
