Tokyo, Japan - January 26, 2024 - Gear up for battle alongside Tanjiro and Nezuko! Bandai Spirits unleashes two new S.H. Figuarts action figures featuring the Demon Slayer siblings, poised for action ahead of the anime's Season 4 premiere.

![](https://i.imgur.com/V1JkIRt.png)

These poseable marvels capture Tanjiro and Nezuko in dynamic combat stances, complete with interchangeable faces and hands for a variety of expressions and battle moves. Fans can even recreate their heartwarming hand-holding scenes from the manga and anime with a special compatibility feature.

![](https://i.imgur.com/ED4lGgX.png)

The details are impressive: Tanjiro comes with four faces, three hand pairs, his iconic Nichirin sword and sheath, and flexible joints for ultimate posing. Nezuko boasts five faces, two hand pairs, additional lower body parts for sitting, and even hidden expressions without her signature bamboo scroll, offering a glimpse into Season 3.

![](https://i.imgur.com/Xpgcu72.png)

Mark your calendars! Pre-orders open February 1st on Tamashii Web, with shipments starting in July for Tanjiro and August for Nezuko. International fans can grab these figures globally through retailers like Amazon and Crunchyroll.

![](https://i.imgur.com/iK6lz5C.png)

But that's not all! To celebrate Season 4, Demon Slayer is hosting a unique global tour for the premiere Episode 1! This special feature-film format stitches together the new episode with the previous season's finale, offering an exclusive pre-screening experience. Limited tickets come with appearances from the Japanese voice actors of Tanjiro and Nezuko, making it a dream event for hardcore fans. Public screenings begin February 21st, 2024, don't miss out!

![](https://i.imgur.com/UxhwB9g.jpeg)

Whether you're a seasoned demon slayer or just beginning your journey, these action figures and the upcoming premiere event offer a fantastic way to dive deeper into the world of Demon Slayer. Get ready to battle alongside Tanjiro and Nezuko, both on your shelf and on the big screen!

![](https://i.imgur.com/5y06WlS.png)

**Key takeaways:**  
* New poseable Tanjiro and Nezuko action figures with interchangeable parts for expression and action.  
* Global release with pre-orders starting February 1st and international retailers like Amazon and Crunchyroll.  
* Special global tour for Season 4 premiere Episode 1, featuring film format and appearances from voice actors.  
* Public screenings of Season 4 begin February 21st, 2024.
