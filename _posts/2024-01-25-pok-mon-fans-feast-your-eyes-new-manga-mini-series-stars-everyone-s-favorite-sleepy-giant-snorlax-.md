Tokyo, Japan - January 18, 2024 - Get ready for a delicious and dreamy Pokémon adventure! Acclaimed manga creator Taku Kuwabara, known for his hit series "Drifting Dragons," has launched a six-chapter mini-series titled "Kabigon no Yume Gourmet" (Snorlax's Dream Gourmet) under the "Project Kabigon" Twitter banner. This exciting project throws the spotlight on everyone's favorite slumbering Pokémon, Snorlax.

The story follows Kumura, a student at Uva Academy, who's tinkering with a fantastical "dream scanner" that translates dreams into sound. Her target? None other than a Snorlax! By feeding it different kinds of food, Kumura observes how the Pokemon's dreams transform, offering a peek into the delectable world of Snorlax's slumbering fantasies.

This mini-series marks a new chapter for the beloved Snorlax, who's receiving his own dedicated franchise project within the vast Pokémon universe. Fans can expect a deep dive into the life and dreams of this iconic Pokemon, all through Kuwabara's signature artistic style.

For those unfamiliar with Kuwabara's work, his "Drifting Dragons" manga has garnered widespread acclaim, boasting a television anime adaptation and dedicated English releases. His talent for blending action, adventure, and humor promises a delightful experience for both dedicated Pokémon fans and newcomers alike.

Here's what awaits you in this Snorlax-centric adventure:

Six chapters of Snorlax's dreamy delights, courtesy of Kumura's "dream scanner."
A deeper exploration of Snorlax's personality and quirks.
Taku Kuwabara's captivating artwork brought to life.
A fun and heartwarming addition to the ever-expanding Pokémon universe.
Mark your calendars, Pokémon trainers! "Kabigon no Yume Gourmet" promises to be a sweet and sleepy treat for the soul. Prepare to delve into the whimsical world of Snorlax's dreams when the first chapter drops on January 19th.
